About Us

RML (Rock Mountain Line) is a family run business based out of Missoula, MT. Founded in 1999, RML prides itself on exceeding customer's expectations. Clark (Matt's Dad), and Seth (Matt's brother), play a crucial role on site each and every day while Stacie (Matt's wife) keeps the business running smoothly in the office. Although RML is contracted out by some of the largest utility companies in western Montana, barely a day goes by where you won’t see Matt down in the trenches with his fellow employees.
